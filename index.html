HOW TO PLAY

Boggle is played with a tray of 16 letter dice, which is shaken to get 16 random letters.
Players have THREE(easy), TWO(medium), or ONE(hard) minute depending on difficulty (shown by the countdown timer)
to find as many words as they can in the grid. Form words by clicking on the letter buttons.

Words are to be formed according to the following rules:

The letters must be adjoining in a 'chain'. (Letter cubes in the chain may be adjacent horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
Words must contain at least three letters.
No letter die may be used more than once within a single word.
A word can only be submitted once, even if it is formed with different dice.

The scoring is as follows:

Fewer than 3 Letters: no score
For words 3 letters or longer, the points given is equal to the length of the submitted word


To submit a word, click the submit word button, or press the right arrow key
If you make a mistake and want to clear the board, press the clear board button, or the left arrow key.
